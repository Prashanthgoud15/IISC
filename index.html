Fostering a Culture of Innovation

IIC PESCE is a dynamic ecosystem dedicated to nurturing creativity and groundbreaking ideas. We empower students to become the next generation of innovators and entrepreneurs.

Our Mission

To create a vibrant local innovation ecosystem and start-up supporting mechanism in the campus. We aim to prepare institute for Atal Ranking of Institutions on Innovation Achievements Framework.

Our Vision

To be a beacon of innovation, guiding students to transform their imaginative ideas into tangible, impactful solutions that address real-world challenges.

Our Initiatives

Ideathons

Brainstorming marathons where brilliant minds collaborate to solve real-world problems and develop groundbreaking concepts.

Workshops

Hands-on sessions on cutting-edge technologies and entrepreneurial skills, led by industry experts and experienced faculty.

Startup Pitching

A platform for aspiring entrepreneurs to present their business ideas to a panel of investors and mentors for funding and guidance.
